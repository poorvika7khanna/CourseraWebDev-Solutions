Watermelon Smoothie

You might think that a watermelon smoothie is going to be a one-dimensional drink. However, this interesting drink builds layers of intriguing flavors on top of the sweet summer melon. It is a perfect example of the difference a few creative additions can make. The lime and mint complement the melon wonderfully, but it's the ginger that gives it a fun contrast that you have to taste to believe.

La Tizana

It's time for a little international influence and la tizana is an amazing fruit smoothie from Venezuela. You can think of it as a nonalcoholic sangria that makes a trip through the blender. This drink uses almost every fruit the market holds, including pineapple, mango, strawberry, grape, banana, and orange. Thus, it makes up for a delicious fruity blend and you would be tempted to have more of it.

Blueberry Kale Smoothie

Thanks to its notoriety as a healthy superfood, kale is a popular option for smoothies. Yet, it can be an acquired taste. The blueberry kale smoothie is the ideal introduction to this distinct flavor. It uses orange juice and a heap of blueberries, which do a wonderful job at You get all the nutrition with a lot more flavor and find yourself coming back for more. An appropriate order after you go for a run!
